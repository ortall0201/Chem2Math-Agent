Core thermodynamic relations
============================

\( \Delta G = -n F E \)

\( \Delta G = \Delta G^\circ + R T \ln Q \)

Goal
----
Derive an expression for the cell potential \( E \) as a function of the standard potential \( E^\circ \), the temperature \( T \), the number of electrons transferred \( n \), and the reaction quotient \( Q \).
